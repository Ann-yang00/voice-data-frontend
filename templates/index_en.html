Audio Recording for Training Set Collection

Please use the following tool to record your voice for each provided sentence, and make sure to fill in your name so that the backend can collect all audio recordings accordingly. The submitted recordings will be used for voice cloning once requested. For best results, please ensure reading clarity and minimum background noise. After pressing the submit button, a confirmation prompt will appear within a few seconds.

On iOS devices, there are interface issues that may cause playback to malfunction. While submitting recordings remains possible, attempting to re-record may result in an error. If you wish to re-record on iOS devices, kindly refresh the page and input your name again before proceeding.
